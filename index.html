Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Embrace the Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR), Advance the Information and Communications Technology (ICT) industry, Support startups, and Promote smart commerce.

Sustainability

Bangladesh 2.0 should be environmentally friendly, and industries should be concerned with maintaining the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and a green ecosystemf

Infrastructure

The Bangladesh 2.0 agenda envisions a future where economic growth is not achieved at the expense of the environment but is instead driven by sustainable practices.

Empowerment

Empowerment is a core pillar in this vision as it seeks to enable individuals and communities to unlock their full potential and actively contribute to the nation's progress.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
